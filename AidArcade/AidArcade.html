Our mission:

We are the Go&Done development team, and we are working on a website that allows people to get urgent help. The help is presented
in the form of free food, medicine, clothing, housing, or even psychological support. Our site can be used by people who are unable to
receive material and non-material assistance for a fee, all services on our site are free and can be accessed by anyone who needs it,
the reason for creating the project is the situation in our country, Ukraine has been suffering from the aggression of the Russian Federation
for three years, many of our citizens are forced to leave their homes and live hundreds of kilometers away from their hometowns, which is why we
launched the AidArcade project to make life easier for people who survived the invasion, saw bloody battles, shelling, some of them lost their
homes, some lost relatives, our project will help our country unite, we really need it now because we are one country and we need to help each other in
this difficult time. Victory is ahead, Glory to Ukraine!
